# Verb to be: personal pronoms

## Observações:

* O pronome I sempre será escrito com letra maiúscula


### Singular

* I
* You
* He
* She
* It

### Plural

* We
* You
* They

## To be in the Present Simple Afirmative

### Singular

* I ***am***
* You ***are***
* He ***is***
* She ***is***
* It ***is***

### Plural

* We ***are***
* You ***are***
* They ***are***
